Get Involved

A free and open Web relies on the active support of the global community.

  • Join the Mozilla Policy & Advocacy mailing list

    This is an email discussion where you can help shape the direction of our policy and advocacy — locally, regionally and globally.

  • Say hello on a Policy & Advocacy Community call

    Join our monthly calls. All are welcome! Ask questions, meet our policy and advocacy community and staff, and find the resources you need.

  • Connect through social media

    Join the conversation on Twitter and stay up to date on the Net Policy blog.

  • Donate to Mozilla

    As a non-profit organization, we depend on the generosity of people like you to fund our work advocating for a free and open Web.
